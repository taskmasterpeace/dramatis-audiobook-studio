# SFX Proof

A sixty-second scene written to exercise the effects layer: rain bed, car door, thunder, gunshot, dog, creaking hinges.

## Proof

The rain hammered the empty parking lot in long grey sheets. Marcus sat in the car a moment longer, watching the warehouse through the streaming windshield. Then he stepped out, and the car door slammed behind him, flat and hard against the storm.

Thunder rolled somewhere far off to the west. He crossed the wet asphalt without hurrying, collar up, water finding its way down his neck anyway.

A gunshot cracked from inside the building. Marcus froze mid-stride. The echo died under the rain and left nothing behind it. Somewhere beyond the fence a dog began to bark, steady and furious.

He reached the side entrance and pushed. The rusted hinges creaked long and slow, and the sounds of the storm fell away behind him.
