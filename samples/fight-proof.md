# Fight Proof

Sixty seconds of alley fight to exercise the house Foley kit — punches, whoosh, body fall, footsteps — narrated for the Battlerap Algorithm voice test.

## The Alley

The footsteps came up fast on the concrete behind him, and Marcus turned just in time. The first swing whistled past his jaw, all air and bad intentions.

He answered with a short right hand, and the punch landed clean. The big man grunted and came back swinging. A second punch caught Marcus under the ribs and folded him half over.

He straightened up slow, spat, and put everything he had left into one more shot. It connected like a door slamming. The big man went down hard onto the concrete, and this time he stayed there.

Marcus stood over him a long moment, breathing hard in the cold. Then he walked away, and his footsteps faded down the alley into the dark.
